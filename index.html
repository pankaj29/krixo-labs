Ask your library

Chat with everything
you've ever read.

Ask a question and Krixo answers from your whole library - or just one book - with tappable citations back to the exact page. Explain a paragraph, screenshot a diagram, or look up a word inline. The clever part: the search runs on your phone, so only your question and the few matching passages ever reach the AI - never your books or files.
