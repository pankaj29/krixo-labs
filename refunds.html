Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated 17 July 2026 · Krixo Labs

This page explains what happens if you want your money back, and how to ask. It expands on section 5 of the Terms of Service and does not contradict it. If the two ever disagree, tell us and we will fix the wording rather than argue about it.

1. What you are buying

Krixo Pro is a 30-day pass, not a subscription. You pay once and get 30 days of unlimited AI, Audio Overview and narration. Your pass activates as soon as the payment is confirmed.

Reading, highlighting, notes, flashcards, vocabulary and search are free and unlimited. You never have to buy Pro to keep using Krixo or to reach your own notes.

The price depends on your country and is shown to you before you pay. A price change never affects a pass you have already bought.

2. Cancellation

There is nothing to cancel. Because Pro is a 30-day pass rather than an auto-renewing subscription, it simply ends. Nothing is charged again unless you choose to buy another pass. We do not store a mandate, and we cannot take money from you without you starting a new payment.

If you stop wanting Pro, do nothing. Your pass runs to its last day and then your account returns to Free, with all of your books, highlights and notes intact.

3. When we refund you

We refund in full, without argument, when:

  • Your payment succeeded but Pro did not activate. Write to us and we will either activate the pass you paid for or refund it, whichever you prefer.
  • You were charged more than once for the same pass. We refund every duplicate.
  • You were charged but never received the pass at all - for example the payment left your account and no pass appears against it.
  • The service was genuinely unusable for the paid features during your pass, because of a fault on our side.

If any of these happened to you, we consider it our mistake to fix, not a favour to grant.

4. When we do not refund

Pro unlocks digital features immediately, so we do not refund a pass you have started using simply because you changed your mind. That is the trade for getting instant access with no trial period and no card kept on file.

Two things that are always true regardless: we refund where the law requires it, and we refund where the service was genuinely unusable. Nothing on this page removes a right your local consumer law gives you.

Deleting your account while a pass is active forfeits the remainder of that pass. Please use it up first, or write to us before you delete.

5. How to ask for a refund

Email support@krixo.in from the address on your Krixo account, and include the payment ID from your Razorpay receipt if you have it. That is all we need to find the transaction.

We aim to reply within 2 working days. There is no form to fill in and no ticket queue to chase. A human reads it.

6. How the money comes back

Payments are handled by Razorpay. We never see or store your card details, and we cannot refund to anywhere other than the method you paid with.

Approved refunds are sent back to your original payment method and typically reach you within 5 to 7 working days, depending on your bank or card issuer. The time is on their side, not ours - once we approve it, we have done what we can.

7. Contact

Refunds, billing questions, complaints and grievances: support@krixo.in. We read everything.
